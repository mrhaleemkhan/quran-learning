FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first class.

Is the 3-day trial really free?
Yes — three full classes, no card details required and no obligation to continue. We want you to be 100% sure before you commit.
What devices and apps do I need?
A laptop, tablet or phone with a stable internet connection. Classes are held over Zoom or Skype with an interactive digital whiteboard — we'll guide you through the simple setup.
Do you offer female teachers for sisters and kids?
Absolutely. We have a dedicated team of female teachers (Ustadhas) for sisters, mothers and children — privacy and comfort are priorities at Ummah Essa.
Can I change my teacher or schedule?
Yes. You can switch teachers, reschedule, pause or cancel at any time directly from your student dashboard or by messaging support.
Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, JazzCash, Easypaisa and bank transfer. International students are billed monthly in USD.
